About Me

I am an AI Engineer and Machine Learning Specialist with a strong background in Generative AI, AI Agents, Transformers, and Diffusion Models. My expertise includes developing AI-powered chatbots, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) models, deep learning pipelines, and scalable data processing systems. With experience in NLP, computer vision, and cloud technologies, I have worked extensively with LangChain, PyTorch, FastAPI, FAISS, and GCP BigQuery. My work involves optimizing deep learning models, designing ETL workflows, and deploying AI applications using Docker and CI/CD. I hold an MSc in Artificial Intelligence and a BSc in Computer Engineering, providing a strong foundation in machine learning, data science, and software engineering. My focus is on advancing Generative AI, transformers, and diffusion models, exploring their applications in real-world problem-solving and innovation.
